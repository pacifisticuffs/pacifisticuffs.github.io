Welcome to Evolution Motorcycles

Evolution Motorcycles was established in 2005 as Evolution Suspension. Originally set up as a specialized suspension and frame workshop it has now become one of the leading full service shops in the Bay Area covering all aspects of motorcycle repair and maintenance. We still offer high quality suspension set up, service and upgrade work but can also ensure the rest of your motorcycle is is up to the same standard and kept that way. We moved into our larger shop back in 2010 and more recently became the South Bay's Aprilia dealer. We also sell used bikes and offer consignment sales.

Our aim at Evolution Motorcycles is to provide a complete quality service in a friendly atmosphere using proven components and quality products to make your riding experience the best it can be.
